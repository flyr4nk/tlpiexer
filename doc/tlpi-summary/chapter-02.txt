CHAPTER 02
FUNDAMENTAL CONCEPTS

OVERVIEW

    This chapter introduces a range of concepts related to Linux system
    programming. It is intended for readers who have worked primarily with
    other operating systems, or who have only limited experience with Linux or
    another UNIX implementation.

SUMMARY

    In this chapter, we surveyed a range of fundamental concepts related to
    Linux system programming. An understanding of these concepts should
    provide readers with limited experience on Linux of UNIX with enough
    background to begin learning system programming.
